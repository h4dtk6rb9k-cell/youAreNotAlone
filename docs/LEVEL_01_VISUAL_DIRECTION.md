# Level 01 Visual Direction

Selected direction: Option 2 - The Window Still Remembers the City.

Preview image: `res://docs/reference/level_01_visual_direction_selected.png`

## Intent

The window is the main emotional light source. The apartment is clean and functioning, but the warmth belongs partly to the city outside, not to the player.

## Required Translation Into Scene

- Keep the fake-isometric / 45-degree room.
- Use warm amber window light as the primary visual path.
- Keep cold grey-green ambient around the room.
- Make the screen a secondary cold flicker, not the dominant light.
- Make the door architecturally logical: wall, frame, and a corridor hint behind it.
- Preserve gameplay layout, collisions, player start, screen interaction, and door interaction.

## Avoid

- Door opening into visual emptiness.
- Flat blockout colors.
- Top-down composition.
- Ruins, dirt, chaos, or abandoned-world language.
